Welcome to Kinko.
A tool for sharing your kinks and roleplaying preferences.

Kinko is designed to offer an easy way to specify your likes, dislikes and requirements for ERP.
Currently it can only output a HTML file. This can be either opened, copied and embedded on any website that allows JavaScript, or shared as a web-page via messaging services such as Discord. Although for security reasons it’s recommended you only open HTML files from people you trust.

In the future, Kinko might expand into something more. With ways of comparing kinks with other people. For the time being it’s just a novel tool for those who have a use for it.

Click the begin button to start. You can hover over buttons to context information, and press the ? button to open the tutorial page if you're stuck.
